Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

At Pleasanton Student Pro, your privacy and security are our top priorities. We believe in complete transparency about how our platform operates.

No Data Collection

We are committed to your privacy:

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • No account creation is required
  • No tracking or analytics are implemented
  • No cookies are used on our platform
How We Keep You Secure

Our platform is designed with security in mind:

  • All connections are encrypted using HTTPS
  • Information is processed locally on your device
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • Regular security audits and updates
Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect any personal information, you maintain complete control over your privacy. You can use our platform with confidence, knowing that your academic activities and usage patterns remain private and secure.
